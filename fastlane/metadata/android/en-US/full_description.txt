VoiceBell is a privacy-focused alarm clock designed to work 100% offline. All voice recognition happens on your device using Vosk - no data is ever sent to servers.

<b>Key Features:</b>

• <b>Offline Voice Commands</b> - Set alarms and timers using natural English commands, completely offline
• <b>Advanced Alarms</b> - Repeating schedules, gradual volume increase, snooze, and customizable tones
• <b>Pre-Alarms for Deep Sleepers</b> - Configure 1-10 gentle pre-alarms before your main alarm
• <b>Timer & Stopwatch</b> - Full-featured timer with lap tracking
• <b>World Clocks</b> - Track time across multiple time zones
• <b>Material Design 3</b> - Beautiful, modern interface with dynamic theming
• <b>100% Privacy</b> - No internet permission, no tracking, no analytics

<b>Privacy Guaranteed:</b>

VoiceBell has NO internet permission. All your data stays on your device:
• Voice recognition uses Vosk (offline speech recognition)
• No Google Play Services required
• Works on degoogled devices (GrapheneOS, LineageOS, etc.)
• Fully open source (GPL-3.0)

<b>Voice Commands:</b>

Simply tap the microphone and say:
• "Set alarm for 7 AM"
• "Wake me at 6:30 AM on weekdays"
• "Set timer for 10 minutes"
• "What alarms are set?"

All voice processing happens locally - your voice data never leaves your phone.

<b>Alarm Features:</b>

• Repeating alarms with weekday selection (Mon-Fri, weekends, custom)
• Gradual volume increase for gentle wake-up
• Pre-alarms with configurable intervals
• Snooze with customizable duration
• Multiple alarm tones
• Per-alarm vibration and flash settings
• Respects system alarm volume
• Full-screen alarm interface

• Deep sleepers who need multiple alarms
